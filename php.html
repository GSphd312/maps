Statistical Spellbreakers Edition!Padlet

Statistical Spellbreakers Edition! is a two-part, wizard-inspired learning activity. Students start with an introductory video that presents magical claims about statistics, then examine the idea hidden behind each illusion. In the second part, they create an eight-second Gemini animation that transforms the correct concept into a short spell-like scene.
