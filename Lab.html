Question Sets

QS3 • Descriptive Statistics Frequencies • Means • SD • Count
Checklist
Guide

Clarify your theme (e.g., obesity, environmental exposure, mental health disparities, chronic disease), identify variables, and run descriptive stats in SPSS. Replicate in Copilot and interpret for two audiences.

Rubric focus: RQ clarity, correct variables, accurate analysis, dual‑audience interpretation, and a thoughtful AI reflection.

QS5 • Relationships One‑sample t‑test • Correlation • Simple Regression
Checklist
Guide

Ideate by selecting and justifying the statistical test (t‑test, correlation, or regression). Then Develop: run SPSS, replicate in Copilot, and write dual‑audience interpretations.

Compare outputs (SPSS vs Copilot) and reflect on clarity, oversimplification, or ethical issues.

QS6 • Group Comparisons Independent/Paired t‑test • ANOVA
Checklist
Guide

Clarify your groups and outcomes. Ideate the correct comparison test (independent/paired t‑test or ANOVA), then Develop: run/replicate and interpret for both audiences.

Document any discrepancies between SPSS and Copilot and how they affect interpretation.

QS7.1 • Categorical Analysis & Synthesis Chi‑square • Final Deliverable
Checklist
Guide

Implement: synthesize findings across QS3/5/6 and create a public‑facing product (policy brief, infographic, slide deck, or AI webpage) tailored to a specific audience.

Include a concise 50‑word summary of purpose, message, and audience.
